Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before booking your escape.

Find clear answers to the most common questions our travelers ask about planning, booking, and traveling with us.

How do I book a trip?

Choose your destination or package, contact our team, and we will guide you through the reservation and confirmation steps.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellation terms depend on the package and travel date. We will share the details with you before confirmation.

Do you offer refunds?

Refund eligibility depends on the package conditions and the timing of the cancellation request.

Are flights included?

Some packages include flights while others can be arranged separately. We will confirm this for each option.

Do I need travel insurance?

Travel insurance is recommended for most trips, especially international travel and adventure packages.

Can I customize my package?

Absolutely. We can tailor each itinerary to suit your preferences, travel style, and budget.

Do you provide visa assistance?

Yes, we can guide you through the general visa information and documentation process for eligible destinations.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers, card payments, and secure online payment options depending on your booking request.

Are children allowed on tours?

Yes, many of our tours are family-friendly. We will recommend the best options for your group size and ages.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we provide competitive pricing and special arrangements for larger group bookings.

Is customer support available 24/7?

Yes, our support team is available around the clock for urgent travel questions and assistance.

Can I change my booking date?

Depending on availability and package terms, date changes may be possible. We will check this for you.

What documents are required?

Passport, visa documents, and any destination-specific requirements may be needed based on your itinerary.

How early should I book?

For the best availability and pricing, booking 2–6 months in advance is ideal for most trips.

Are meals included?

Meal inclusion depends on the package. Some include full-board service while others offer flexible dining options.

Still have questions?

Our travel experts are always ready to help.
